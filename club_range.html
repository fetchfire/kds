Club Range

The club range is available to club members. Talk to Alan Jones at a club meeting for more infomation. Please respect the range rules while you are at the range.

Range Rules:
  • Contact Alan Jones to check whether the Range is open.
  • 10.00am to 2.30pm Saturday & Sunday only. You must vacate by 2.30pm for the afternoon milking.
  • Landowner may at times, due to ground conditions close the farm to all vehicles. Please respect this.
  • You must have your Membership Card with you while using the Range, but DO NOT police others.
  • Do not place targets on the fence.
  • Please take your rubbish home with you, this includes used targets.
  • Respect the fact you are on private land. It is a privilege for the club to have access to the range, not a right!
