Welcome to Kapiti Deerstalkers

A member of the New Zealand Deerstalkers Association

The New Zealand Deerstalkers Association is a national body committed to the promotion of hunting as a sport. Its mission statement is:

"to retain, enhance and create opportunities for the enjoyment of legitimate recreational hunting and the sport of shooting, for the members of the Association"

It is active on many fronts of interest to the hunter, such as wild animal management, access and conservation issues, firearms legislation etc. The Association administers national trophy, photographic and shooting competitions for members from over 50 branches throughout New Zealand. It has also arranged public liability insurance for members.

The Kapiti Branch (Kapiti Deerstalkers) primarily serves the Kapiti Coast and Horowhenua areas, although members come from throughout the greater Wellington region.
